Welcome to the unofficial Fistful Of Frags wiki

Fistful of Frags is a multiplayer first-person shooter game developed as a modification (mod) for the video game "Half-Life 2." It was originally released in 2007 by the Fistful of Frags Team. It was released on Steam for Microsoft Windows, Mac OS and Linux on May 9, 2014, with several updates subsequently adding features and maps.

The game is set in the American Wild West during the late 19th century and features gameplay focused on gunfights and dueling. Players can choose from a variety of Western-themed weapons, such as revolvers, rifles, shotguns, and dynamite, to engage in fast-paced battles across different maps.
